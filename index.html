Private Homeschool • Grades K–12

Baumert Academy

Baumert Academy is a private, family-run homeschool serving students from kindergarten through 12th grade. Instruction is parent/teacher-led and paced to the learner.

High school students may have the opportunity to participate in dual enrollment classes at nearby institutions, where available and eligible.

About

Our homeschool maintains a straightforward, supportive learning environment with clear expectations and regular progress checks. Coursework, reading, and projects are documented simply and accurately.

K–5

Foundations in reading, writing, and mathematics; age-appropriate science and social studies; daily reading.

  • Phonics and handwriting practice
  • Number sense and arithmetic
  • Short lessons and review

6–8

Composition, pre-algebra/algebra, general science, world & U.S. history; study skills.

  • Structured writing and projects
  • Organizational habits and notes
  • Regular feedback and revision

9–12

Literature and composition, algebra through advanced math as appropriate, lab sciences, civics/economics.

  • Course documentation and transcripts
  • Independent reading & research
  • Dual enrollment when eligible

Enrollment & Records

Attendance, coursework logs, and periodic summaries are maintained for family records and planning.

  • Attendance & coursework logs
  • Grading as appropriate to course
  • Quarterly summary notes
