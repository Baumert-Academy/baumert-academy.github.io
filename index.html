Private Homeschool • Grades K–12

Focused, family-run learning from kindergarten through 12th grade.

Baumert Academy is a private, family-run homeschool. Instruction is personalized to the student and paced appropriately for growth.

High school students may pursue dual enrollment classes at nearby institutions, earning both high school and college credit where available.

About

We provide a straightforward, supportive learning environment with clear expectations and steady progress. Parents/teachers guide coursework and projects, and we keep documentation simple and accurate.

K–5

Reading, writing, math foundations; nature study; basic science and history.

  • Foundational literacy & numeracy
  • Short lessons, practice, and review
  • Hands-on activities as appropriate

6–8

Composition, pre-algebra/algebra, general science, world and U.S. history.

  • Study skills and organization
  • Incremental writing and projects
  • Regular feedback and revision

9–12

Literature & composition, algebra through advanced math as appropriate, lab sciences, civics/economics.

  • Transcript and course documentation
  • Independent reading & research
  • Dual enrollment available when eligible

Enrollment & Records

We keep simple records of completed work and maintain clear communication with parents/guardians.

  • Attendance and coursework logs
  • Grading as appropriate to course
  • Quarterly summary notes
